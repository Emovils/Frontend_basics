How to Cook My Favorite Meal


Fried Rice and Salad


Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Choice Rice
  • 1kg Protein of chioce(Fish, Beef, Turkey, Chicken, Goat meat)
  • Fried Rice Spices Ball pepper( Red,Green,Yellow) Green Peas Carrot Liver Fresh Prawns(Opt) Sweet Corn Cucumber Cabbage(Purple/White) Condensed milk Onions Mayonnaise of choice
  • 2 cups of vegetable oil
  • Cooking Magrine
  • Chicken/fried Rice Seasoning Curry Ginger/Garlic Chicken spices local Mixed Spices(opt) Fried Rice Spices Dried onions Yoghurt(opt For chicken seasoning)
  • Salt and Maggi of choice


Steps


  1. Marinate the chicken with Soy sauce, Yoghurt, Salt, Maggi, Chicken spices, Habanero pepper, Ginger/ Garlic etc cover and Keep for About 10mins or Overnigh
  2. Wash the Rice and Keep in a Sieve
  3. Pour the Meatstock into the Pot and add Water and Spices to Taste
  4. Add the Washed Rice and Allow To Cook
  5. Pour the Vegetable Oil Into a Pan and Fry the Protein
  6. Once the Rice Is Ready, Wash a Sparate Pan and Heat
  7. Add Margine to the Already Heated Pan, Pour your Already chopped Fried Rice Spices and add Seasoning to Taste
  8. Fry for 10mins After which you Mix with the Rice and Fry in Batches
  9. Dress the Salade in a Bowl and put in the Fridge to Cool
  10. Clean up the Cooking Area
